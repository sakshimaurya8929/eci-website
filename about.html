About ECI Institute

ECI Institute of Management & Technology (Entry Cadder Intersection) is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education in the fields of Management, Technology, and Computer Applications.

Established with a vision to create skilled professionals, our institute offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma courses. We believe in a holistic approach to education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical training.

Our institute is equipped with modern infrastructure including well-furnished classrooms, advanced computer laboratories, a rich library, and dedicated placement cell to ensure the overall development of our students.
