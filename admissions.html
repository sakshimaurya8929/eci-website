Admission Process

Simple steps to get admitted at ECI Institute

1️⃣

Step 1: Enquiry

Visit our campus or contact us online to enquire about the courses you are interested in.

2️⃣

Step 2: Fill Application

Fill the admission application form available at the institute or download from our website.

3️⃣

Step 3: Submit Documents

Submit required documents along with the application form to the admission office.

4️⃣

Step 4: Pay Fees

Pay the admission fees through cash, cheque, or online payment methods.

5️⃣

Step 5: Confirmation

Receive your admission confirmation letter and student ID card.

6️⃣

Step 6: Start Classes

Attend the orientation program and begin your academic journey with us.
