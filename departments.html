Academic Departments

ECI Institute has 3 dedicated departments

💻 Department of Information Technology (IT)

The IT Department is the backbone of our institute, offering cutting-edge education in computer science, software development, web technologies, and information systems. Our students gain practical knowledge through hands-on lab sessions, live projects, and industry internships.

Courses under this department: BCA, MCA, O Level, ADCA, DCA, Triple C (CCC)

Key Subjects: Programming Languages (C, C++, Java, Python), Database Management, Web Development, Networking, Data Structures, Operating Systems, Software Engineering.

Facilities: 3 Advanced Computer Labs, Internet Lab, Project Development Room, Smart Classrooms.

🔧 Department of Industrial Training (ITI)

The ITI Department focuses on providing vocational and technical training to students. This department prepares students for industrial jobs by offering hands-on training in various trades and technical skills. Our workshops are equipped with modern tools and machinery.

Key Areas: Electrical Training, Mechanical Workshop, Fitter Trade, Electronics, Welding, and Plumbing.

Facilities: Fully equipped workshops, practical training centers, and industry visit programs.

Goal: To create skilled technicians and tradespeople who are ready for the manufacturing and service industries.

🏥 Department of Health & Paramedical (DHP)

The DHP Department offers courses in health sciences and paramedical studies. This department trains students to become healthcare professionals with the knowledge and skills required in hospitals, clinics, and healthcare organizations.

Key Areas: Medical Lab Technology, Nursing Assistance, Health & Hygiene, First Aid Training, Patient Care Management.

Facilities: Medical lab, anatomy models, first aid training room, clinical practice facilities.

Goal: To produce competent healthcare workers who can contribute to the nation's health infrastructure.
